Prints "Hello, World!" multiple times if the EULA is accepted.
Usage

    Ensure EULA.txt exists in the same directory.

	Run:

./helloworldprog        # checks EULA
./helloworldprog 1      # bypass EULA

    Enter number of repetitions (0 to exit).

Requirements

    Standard C++ libraries

Notes

    EULA is accepted if the last word of EULA.txt is true or yes.

    Prints messages with a 0.5-second delay.
